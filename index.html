About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense action survival game where you must fend off endless waves of monsters. Use your skills and upgrades to survive as long as possible in this challenging environment.

This addictive game combines fast-paced action with strategic decision-making, offering hours of gameplay as you try to beat your high score with each run.

How To Play

Game Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Abilities trigger automatically when in range of enemies
  • Collect experience gems to level up
  • Choose upgrades wisely to build a powerful character

Survival Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize offensive abilities early game
  • Balance your build between offense and defense
  • Focus on surviving the first few minutes

Game Features

Multiple Abilities

Unlock and upgrade various abilities to create your unique build.

Level Progression

Gain experience and level up to become stronger as waves get harder.

Endless Challenge

See how long you can survive against increasingly difficult enemies.
